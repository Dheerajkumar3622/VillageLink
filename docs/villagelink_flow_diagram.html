ğŸ“– 9. Detailed User Tutorials

ğŸŒ¾ For Kisan (Farmers)

  • Mandi Rates: Go to GramMandi to see live prices for Wheat, Rice, and Vegetables from nearby mandis.
  • Dr. Kisan AI: Take a photo of a sick leaf. The AI will diagnose the disease and suggest the right pesticide.
  • Cold Storage: Book space in local cold storages to prevent crop rot during low price seasons.
  • Mandi Transport: Book a tractor or mini-truck (CargoLink) to transport your harvest directly from the farm.

ğŸª For Vendors (Shops/Dhabas)

  • Digital Storefront: List your items in FoodLink. Add photos and prices for village customers.
  • Digital Khata: Keep track of customer credits (Udhari) digitally. No more paper notebooks!
  • Hygiene Audit: Use the AI scanner to upload photos of your kitchen. High hygiene scores attract more customers.
  • Order Management: Get real-time alerts for new orders. Mark them 'Ready' so passengers can pick them up when the bus arrives.

ğŸš— For Drivers (Bus/Jeep/Truck)

  • Subscription: Pay a small monthly fee to access infinite passenger leads without commission.
  • CargoLink: If you have empty space, list it under 'Cargo'. Earn extra by carrying small parcels.
  • Route Optimization: Follow the AI-suggested route to find more passengers in intermediate villages.
  • Drowsiness Alert: Keep your phone mounted. The app will alert you if it senses unsafe driving patterns.

ğŸ“¦ For Logistics Partners

  • Parcel Hub: Manage incoming and outgoing parcels for your village.
  • Fleet Management: Track all your vehicles in one place with the Operator Dashboard.
  • UMG Grid: Connect with other transport providers to move parcels across long distances seamlessly.

ğŸ‘¤ For Passengers (Village Citizens)

  • Smart Booking: Select your route and book tickets via UPI or GramCoin wallet.
  • Guardian Mode: Share your live trip with family. If the vehicle deviates, they are alerted instantly.
  • Food on the Go: Order food from dhabas on your route. It will be ready when your bus reaches.
  • GramScore: Regular travelers earn GramCoins which can be used for free rides later.
